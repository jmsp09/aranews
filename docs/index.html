Privacy Policy for Aranews

Last updated: April 02, 2026

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our mobile application Aranews.

1. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our service, we do not require you to provide us with any personally identifiable information. The application uses public data from Aragón Open Data.

2. Log Data and Local Storage

The application stores locally on your device:

  • The latest downloaded news to enable fast reading (SQLite/Room database).
  • The date and time of the last update (SharedPreferences).

This data remains exclusively on your device and is not sent to external servers or shared with us.

3. Service Providers and Third-Party Links

The application uses the following services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: For distribution and basic store analytics.
  • Aragón Open Data API: To retrieve public news content.
  • Chrome Custom Tabs (Android Browser): When you click on a news link, an integrated browser opens. We do not control the privacy policies of the external websites you visit through these links.

4. Security

We value your trust, which is why we do not collect sensitive personal data. However, remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through the official app store channels.
